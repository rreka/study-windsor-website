What we do

Welcome to the University of Windsor open science study group! We are a community of scientists, students, researchers and librarians who support each other in using computing skills for science and research.

Skill Sharing

Share your favorite coding skills and tools with your friends and colleagues in friendly, no-pressure work-alongs.

Co-Working

Get together to work on your coding projects, help each other out and share your work.

Community Building

Meet new people in your field, organization or community - and find out what we can do when we work together.

Co-working

Location: Biology Learning Centre (Biology Building 201)
Date & time: Every other Thursday, 2:00-3:30 pm

Bring your work, your laptop and your code and let's work together. This is a place to work on your project and ask each other questions as needed. No TAs - just mutual support from folks working on related topics.

Join us

Joining our study group is easy. We ask that everyone abide by our Code of Conduct. Just subscribe to our email list in order to post questions and contribute your knowledge on our virtual space. Drop-by any of our co-working session without registration. We're happy to have you!
