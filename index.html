UWindsor Science Coding Study Group

An open science study group for, and by, UWindsor researchers & students.

We are a community of scientists, students, researchers and librarians who support each other in using computing skills for science and research.

Skill Sharing

Share your favourite coding skills and tools with your friends and colleagues in friendly, no-pressure work-alongs.

Co-Working

Get together to work on your coding projects, help each other out and share your work.

Community Building

Meet new people in your field, organization or community - and find out what we can do when we work together.

In-person events

Join our study group in person as we work on our projects together, and attend related workshops. All events are free to join. Simply drop-in during any one of our co-working sessions, and sign-up for the free Library workshops. Check out the calendar for all events and details.

Co-working

Bring your work, your laptop and your code and let's work together. This is a place to work on your project and ask each other questions as needed. No TAs - just mutual support from folks working on related topics.

Location: Herbarium (Biology building, second floor)
Date & time: Thursdays, 1:00 - 3:00 pm

Workshops

In collaboration with this study group, Leddy Library hosts a series of data science workshops. Workshops cover introductory topics in R, Python, SQL Git. Find dates & descriptions on the calendar. Register for the workshops with the Library.

Location: Leddy Library
Date & time: Winter 2020: TBA

Virtually

Join our study group community virtually and share your favourite tools and skills, ask questions about coding problems you're running into with your research, and find out what other people are up to.

Slack

We maintain a Slack workspace to stay connected. This is open to anyone at UWindsor to join, at no cost.
